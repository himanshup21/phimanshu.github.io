DS 216: Machine Learning for Data Science (MLDS)

Class Timing: Tue-Thu 2:00 pm - 3:30 pm 
Teams Link: 

Description

This four credits course aims to cover machine learning techniques and statistical methods required for planning, developing and evaluating methods, especially applicable for various data analysis tasks. The course would also require students to implement programming assignments/projects related to these topics.

Academic Integrity and Ethical Conduct

In this course, we hold dear the principles of academic integrity, expecting every student to embrace the responsibility of producing original work and recognizing the contributions of others through proper citation. Let it be clear that plagiarism, cheating on exams, and any other acts of academic dishonesty are serious offenses that not only compromise your educational development but also undermine the trust and respect essential to the scholarly community. Such actions are contrary to the values of our institution and will result in stringent consequences, which may include failing grades or further disciplinary measures.
